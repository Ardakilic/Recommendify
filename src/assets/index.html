Recommendify2

Use this tool to make a playlist for Spotify from your Last.fm recommendations, Band name, obsessions or Tag (genre)

Your credentials are to fetch the playlist data, and they're NEVER stored!

Success!

Copy the data below to your clipboard, then Open your Spotify client, and paste the content into the playlist directly. You'll see the new songs will be added to your playlist. You can repeat the fetching and get new recommendations, and thus, can create longer playlists.
